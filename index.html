Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

PlugZen is built to be local-first and private by design. It runs entirely on your device, makes zero network requests, and has no servers, accounts, analytics, or ads.

What PlugZen accesses

  • Your installed extensions Via the management permission — names, icons, versions, and enabled state — so PlugZen can list and toggle them.

What PlugZen stores

  • Profiles, schedules, tags & settings Stored in chrome.storage.sync, which Chrome may sync across the devices you're signed into.
  • Install snapshot & scheduler activity log Stored in chrome.storage.local — on this device only, never synced or transmitted.

What PlugZen does not do

  • ✓Does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.
  • ✓Does not contact any external server, analytics, or tracking service.
  • ✓Does not read the content of the web pages you visit.
  • ✓Has no accounts, no ads, and includes no remote code.

Permissions, explained

PermissionWhy it's needed
managementList your extensions and enable/disable them — the core function.
storageSave your profiles, schedules, tags, and settings.
alarmsFire scheduled enable/disable rules without a persistent background process.
notificationsTell you when a scheduled rule or bulk action runs.
contextMenusAdd the right-click "disable this page's extensions" action.
Host permissionsNone requested. PlugZen has no access to any website.

Your control over your data

  • Export anytime Download your full configuration as a JSON file from Options → Import / Export.
  • Delete anytime Remove the extension, or clear your profiles, schedules, and tags from the Options page.
